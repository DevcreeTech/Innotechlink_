FAQs

Check out our FAQ section for answers to common questions.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I use InnoTech on mobile devices?

Yes, InnoTech is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices with a modern web browser.

How do I upgrade my subscription?

To upgrade your subscription, go to Account Settings > Subscription and select your desired plan.
